Get Your Tickets

Tickets are available for purchase online and at the venue. Prices are as follows:

  • General Admission: $20
  • Student Admission: $10
  • VIP Admission: $50

Ticket Details

Our tickets are categorized to suit every need:

  • General Admission: Provides entry to all events with unreserved seating.
  • Student Admission: Discounted entry for students with valid ID, including unreserved seating.
  • VIP Admission: Includes reserved seating, access to the VIP lounge, and complimentary refreshments.

How to Purchase

Purchasing tickets is simple and can be done online or at the venue. We recommend purchasing online to ensure you secure your spot:

Online Purchase

  • Click on the "Buy Tickets Online" button below.
  • Fill out the registration form with your details.
  • Select the events you wish to attend.
  • Make your payment securely through our portal.

Offline Purchase at VIT Main Building

If you prefer to purchase tickets in person, follow these steps:

  • Visit the VIT Main Building during working hours.
  • Proceed to the ticket counter located on the ground floor.
  • Provide your details to the staff at the counter.
  • Select the events you wish to attend.
  • Make your payment at the counter (cash or card accepted).
  • Collect your tickets and receipt.
